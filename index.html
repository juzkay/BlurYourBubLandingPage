Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I blur kids' faces before posting?

+

Blurring isn't just a filter—it's bulletproof privacy. Full-face blur stops identity linkage for good—and BlurYourBub makes that happen completely on-device before any sharing.

When a video or photo is loaded in the app, does it go anywhere?

+

No, your photos and videos never leave your device. BlurYourBub processes everything locally on your phone. When you load media into the app, it stays completely private and secure on your device. The app creates a new, blurred version that you can save separately, while your original remains untouched in your camera roll.

Can't AI remove these blurs?

+

No! BlurYourBub creates a flat file with no layers, making it impossible for AI to reverse or remove the blur effects. Once processed, the blur is permanently embedded in the image.

Isn't putting a sticker or emoji on it through Instagram enough?

+

Not quite. Stickers may look safe, but your original unblurred images may still be uploaded or exist in the platfroms cloud backups, chats, or shared folders on social platforms. On-device blurring means only the redacted version ever gets uploaded or shared—keeping your child private from the start. BlurYourBub ensures the blur is applied BEFORE upload.

Is there any statistic around this?

+

Absolutely—1 in 8 children worldwide have been victims of image-based abuse in just the past year. That's nearly 302 million impacted globally—highlighting the urgency and effectiveness of full-face blurring as a protective measure. Source

Can I adjust the blur intensity?

+

Yes! The app offers multiple blur levels from light blur to heavy pixelation, giving you complete control over how much you want to obscure faces while maintaining photo quality.

Does the app work offline?

+

Absolutely! BlurYourBub works completely offline. All face blurring happens directly on your device, ensuring your photos remain private and the app works anywhere, even without internet.
